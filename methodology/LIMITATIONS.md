# Known Limitations — FarmBlock FDI v2.0
**Agent:** PROOF | **Date:** 2026-04-17 | **Standard:** Peer-review ready

1. **USDA FARA vintage (2019):** Food access conditions may have changed. ACS proxy substituted.
2. **CDC PLACES modeled estimates:** Not direct measurement. Small-area estimation model.
3. **D4 health imputed to 0:** Where CDC unavailable — conservative, may understate distress.
4. **Equal weights (v1 baseline):** Not empirically validated. PCA weighting recommended for v2.
5. **50 counties only:** Not nationally representative. National coverage planned for v3.0.
6. **Correlation only:** No causal claims. FDI maps co-occurrence, not causation.
7. **ACS 5-year rolling:** Not single-year snapshot — temporal smoothing applies.
8. **Food desert proxy:** ACS threshold approximation of USDA LILA — labeled on all rows.

*These limitations do not diminish utility. Stating them demonstrates scientific integrity.*
